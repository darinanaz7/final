MICROBIOLOGICAL LABORATORY

The microbiological laboratory is equipped with modern equipment, Culture Media, test systems, reagents and two microbiological computer analyzers: Micro-Tax (Austria) and Mini API (France). Allows you to identify a wide range of pathogens of infectious and purulent-inflammatory diseases, as well as quickly identify clinically important facultative-anaerobic bacteria in case of urgent need (within 3-5 hours).

Jane Doe

Head of the microbiological laboratory

The microbiological laboratory is part of the diagnostic services of JSC "DMC".

  • Established in 2001.
  • The action is located in a separate building on the territory of the hospital complex.
  • Permission of the regime Commission to work with infectious material of pathogenicity groups III-IV No. kz08vmy00003039 dated 26.04.2022.
  • The laboratory's activities are carried out in clinical, scientific and educational areas.
  • The laboratory includes a Department of bacteriological and serological diagnostics.

The microbiological laboratory is equipped with modern equipment, Culture Media, test systems, reagents and three microbiological computer analyzers: Vitek-2 Compact (Biomerieux Russia) and Bact/Allert (Biomerieux Russia), which makes it possible to identify a wide range of pathogens of infectious and purulent-inflammatory diseases, as well as, if urgently needed, conduct rapid identification (within 3-5 hours) and sensitivity of clinically important facultative-anaerobic bacteria to antibiotics.

  • Good material and technical base: (microbiological computer analyzers" Vitek Compact", automatic bacteriological analyzer of blood cultures bact/ALERT® 3D, reagents, Metrological control).
  • Personnel potential (all doctors and laboratory assistants have the highest category and have been working for more than 15 years).
  • The use of standardized antibiotic sensitivity determination and testing methods (EUCAST).
  • Conduct regular intra-laboratory monitoring.
  • VOC-participation in External international quality control and inter-laboratory comparison studies.
  • The WHONET program, mis and Fox systems were also introduced.

*Vitek 2 Compact Analyzer is a fully automatic system that provides the detection of microorganisms and their antimicrobial sensitivity in one day.

*The system is designed to detect gram-negative Bacillus, gram-positive cocci, anaerobic bacteria, neisseries, hemophilic Bacillus, other stubborn bacteria, Corynebacteria, lactobacilli, Bacillus, fungi (more than 450 taxa).

* Advanced Expert System the built-in expert system automatically checks each study result for sensitivity, providing a specific phenotype profile, indicating the resistance mechanism/s for each isolate.

* BACT / ALERT® 3D - automatic bacteriological analyzer of blood cultures.
