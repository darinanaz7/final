About Us

Learn more
Darina Clinic has more than 20 years of experience in providing multidisciplinary medical services using high technologies in the market of Kazakhstan and has reached the international level.

DMC JSC focuses its activities on the market of medical services using high technologies and the development of science in priority areas of healthcare in the Republic of Kazakhstan. We are the main center in the Republic of Kazakhstan for the provision of arrhythmological medical services, cardiac surgery, therapeutic, cellular technologies, etc.

In addition, our center is developing a multidisciplinary approach that ensures that the patient can receive the necessary services in one place and in the shortest possible time.

The center specializes in such branches of medicine as:

  • Cardiac surgery for adults and children;
  • Surgery;
  • lar technologies;
  • Arrhythmology;
  • Cardiology;
  • Therapy;
  • Gynecology;
  • Urology;
  • Interventional cardiology;
  • Neurosurgery.
